Welcome to the classic Tic Tac Toe game! Hereâ€™s how to play:

  • The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  • Player X always goes first.
  • Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in empty cells.
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins!
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a draw.
  • Click "New Game" to reset the score or "Reset Game" to restart without resetting the score.
  • You can mute/unmute sound effects using the button at the bottom.
